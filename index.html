🌍 Why Trees Are Important

Trees give us fresh air, shade, and a cleaner environment. Planting more trees helps animals, humans, and the Earth stay healthy.

Nature is very important for our future. Small actions like saving water, recycling, and planting trees can make a big difference.

☀ Quick Eco Tips

  • 🌱 Plant more trees
  • 💧 Save water daily
  • ♻ Recycle plastic and paper
  • 🚲 Walk or cycle when possible
